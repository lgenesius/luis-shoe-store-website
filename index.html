About Luis Shoe Store

Luis Shoe Store founded in early 2020 by Luis Genesius and located in the city of Jakarta, Indonesia. Luis Shoe Store works closely with well-known and local brands, therefore the products sold by Luis Shoe Store are original brands that have partnered together.

The aims of the Luis Shoe Store is that people can get authentic goods from well-known and local brands at standard prices and help introduce local brands so that local brands can grow and participate in advancing the fashion industry in Indonesia.
